Translation Nations


How To Play:

Translation Nations is a game which uses an API (Application Programming Interface) to collect the titles of nations from around the world in 30 different languages. Your job is to guess which nation (in English) matches the translated text, before your time runs up.

We'll keep track of your score, so you can review your answers afterwards. If you get stuck, feel free to change the language or use the "hint?" button. However, using the hint button will take 5 seconds off your time, so use it sparingly!

Good luck!
