How To Play:

Model Citizen is a trivia game that uses an API (Application Programming Interface) to collect the titles of nations from around the world in 30 different languages. Your job is to guess which nation (in English) matches the translated title before the time runs up.

We'll keep track of your score so you can review your answers and winrate after you're done playing. If you get stuck, feel free to change the language or use the "hint?" button. However, using the hint button will take 5 seconds off your time, so use it sparingly!

Good luck!
