In 2022, human beings are closer than we have ever been. In an instant, we can communciate with one another, regardless of who we are or where we may be living. Through shared information, empathy, and the innovations of modern technology we are slowly moving towards becoming one global super-culture.

But how well do we know each other, really? If we are to be model global citizens, one should think the answer would be "well". Luckily for you, we made a game to test that question. How well do you know the nations of the world?

How To Play:

Model Citizen is a trivia game that uses an API (Application Programming Interface) to collect the images of flags from more than 200 nations around the world. Your job is to guess the corresponding titles (in English) of as many flags as possible before the time runs out. 10 seconds will be added to the timer for every correct answer you submit.

We'll keep track of your score so you can review your answers and win rate after you're done playing. If you get stuck, feel free to use the "hint?" button or click "submit" to move onto the next flag. However, submitting an incorrect answer will take 5 seconds off your time, so keep your eye on the clock.

Good luck, citizen!
